Ribeye Steak

Description

Nothing says a romantic dinner like a good ribeye. Or maybe you felt that you deserved one! No judgements here.

Ingredients

  • Ribeye Steak
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Season the steak liberally with salt and pepper.
  2. Heat a skillet/pan to 400 degrees Fahrenheit and add an oil of your choice.
  3. Sear one side of the steak for a little over a minute for a perfect medium rare.
  4. Sear the other side and baste with butter and aromatics of your choice.
  5. Let it rest for at least two minutes.
  6. Now enjoy your glorious steak. Savor it well.
