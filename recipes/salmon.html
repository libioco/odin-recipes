Broiled Salmon

Description

This is a very delicious and healthy meal to make for lunch or even dinner! It requires very little ingredients and is very easy to prepare. Just add some rice and it makes for the perfect post-gym meal!

Ingredients

  • Salmon Fillet
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Garlic Powder
  • Cherry Tomatoes (optional)

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees Fahrenheit.
  2. Prepare an oven-safe dish with aluminum foil. This makes cleaning up easier later.
  3. Season with a liberal amount of salt, pepper, and garlic power.
  4. Spray a thin coat of vegetable oil to prevent the seasoning from burning (optional)
  5. Bake the salmon for 15-20 minutes.
  6. Move the salmon to the top rack and broil for five minutes.
  7. Done!
